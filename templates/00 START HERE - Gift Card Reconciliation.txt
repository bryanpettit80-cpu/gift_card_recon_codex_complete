GIFT CARD RECONCILIATION - QUICK GUIDE
=======================================

WEEKLY RECONCILIATION
1. Save one Monday-Sunday Gift Card Activity report in the correct inbox:
   01 Weekly Gift Card Activity Reports\9354 Richmond\activity
   01 Weekly Gift Card Activity Reports\9355 Virginia Beach\activity
2. Close the Activity report and any prior reconciliation workbook in Excel.
3. Double-click: Run Weekly Gift Card Reconciliation.cmd
4. Review the store status and finished-report path shown on screen.

The weekly program obtains the matching POS and tender data automatically.
Do not create or edit POS control CSV files. A valid completed week is archived
with its Activity report, extracted POS evidence, workbook, and hash manifest.
Completed weekly workbooks, including REVIEW, remain under 03 Finished Reports\Weekly.

MONTHLY CLOSE
1. Save each new Darden credit memo PDF in:
   02 Monthly Close Inputs\Darden Reports - Drop Here
2. Close prior monthly-close workbooks and PDFs.
3. Double-click: Run Monthly Gift Card Close.cmd
4. Review each store's CLOSED, CLOSED WITH REVIEW, or REVIEW REQUIRED status.

Blocked monthly-close diagnostics are placed in:
   03 Finished Reports\Monthly Close - Review Required

IMPORTANT
- Do not edit Gift Card Reconciliation Automation or _automation_runs.
- Do not move or rename completed evidence under 04 Archive.
- An empty weekly inbox is normal and produces no report.
- If the screen says ATTENTION NEEDED, stop and review the exact message.
- The program never invents missing POS values and never publishes incomplete evidence.
- Run Check Gift Card Reconciliation Health.cmd after a program update or if
  Dropbox, Excel, Micros, or the operator runtime appears unavailable.
